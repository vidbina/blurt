“Stay outta trouble,”
momma said as momma sighed

Her fear: her youngest son
be a victim of homicide

But I gotta get you outta here momma,
or I’mma die
…inside

And either way, you lose me momma
so let loose of me

I got the rein
our direction will soon change

Sean “Jay-Z” Carter

These lines in No Hook apply to different lives in different forms. Some don’t risk homicides, but burn-outs. Some don’t need to be told to stay “out of trouble”, but “out of stress”.

It tells the tales of sons and daughters fighting to the death, as in giving every drop of energy available… even if it is just to provide themselves the inner peace of having tried everything within reach to provide their loved ones and themselves an alternative to “being broke”.

I know a few…

Momma tells me not to get overworked. She obviously wants me around for a long time… question is… would I be content with being around for a long time under any condition? Any condition that allows me to proudly say “momma, I made it” will do.

The rest… Unacceptable!
