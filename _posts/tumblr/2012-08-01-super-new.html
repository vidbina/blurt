suprnovae:

We have just registered our very own shortlink, urlhack, if you will, which should make finding us online much easier for you.

It is most likely known to you that a supernovae is a rather impressive stellar explosion. We’re extremely fond of things that cause big bangs and therefore chose this name to suggest that we intend to influence our surroundings in a similar fashion.

The word’s etymology leads us to the Latin Super and Novae and our buzzing minds decided that nu, pronounced as “new”, would be the perfect TLD in our case.

Our name is comprised of the latin super meaning “above, beyond” and novae meaning “new” and now you can find us online by using the URL “supr dot new”.

http://supr.nu — You have to admit… that is supr awesome!
