This is where the resourcefulness of men shines — instruments from garbage. If there’s a will… Favio Chavez and his colleagues are doing great work, teaching music with self-made instruments on the garbagedumps of Bolivia or Paraguay… (forgot the exact country as I was too captivated by the instruments).

They sounded good too!
