Robert Glasper did it again. This guys is really flying ever higher than before.

The voices of Common and Jill Scott add some of the wonderful colors on Glasper's album.

It is truly remarkable how Glasper consistently succeeds in composing very “simple” songs which sound so full and awesome. He makes it look so easy – like he's effortlessly shaking these remarkable sounds from his sleeves through his board.

Glasper has been one of my study subjects for a while now. Every time I listen I hear new things. I've also witness him play live, on what may be considered one of his least better days (he was probably a bit stressed maybe even frustrated as the main performer of the night had missed his flight). Yet whilst stressed he still managed to dazzle the audience with his musical genius.

Do keep those experiments going…
