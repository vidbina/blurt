We’re approaching Rotterdam as my fingers race against time to hammer this message unto my screen. Just had a great few chats at a Appsterdam Meet & Drink gathering at Cafe Bax. Spoke with one of the New Lemurs devs about their approach and connected with other entrepreneurial dreamers and tinkerers.

I would definitely recommend entrepreneurial minds to make the time to attend one of these meetups (somewhere in their lifetime). The stories from fellow megaminds are inspiring. I did the chatting over a couple glasses of water, chicken sates, fries and a beer. Yup, the meetup joint serves decent food.

Straight from a client near Rotterdam to Amsterdam, for a bit to eat and a couple of nourishing conversations and now I’m headed back home.

Good night! Great folks! I’m aching to discover more great minds and unearth other inspiring stories from Appsterdammers in the near future ;)
