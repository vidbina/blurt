I have been playing with ableton live for a few weeks now and I'd like to share a few things or two.

My fairly basic setup:
- yamaha motif es8 (main board)
- laptop (sequencer)
- edirol pc-50 (midi board)

In most cases I wouldn't really need a clicktrack but sometimes (laziness might also be a factor) I'd rather track a few parts for a live gig. The only parts I actually play live are the piano parts (if there are any). I'm still learning organ techniques but if the situation would ask for it I would be able to lay down an organ line - not the best though.
